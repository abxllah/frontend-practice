Student Media Festival 2025

The Student Media Festival 2025 is a vibrant celebration of creativity, innovation, and storytelling. It brings together young talents from across schools and universities to showcase their skills in film, photography, digital art, animation, and journalism. This festival provides students with a platform to express their ideas, share their perspectives, and connect with like-minded individuals. By participating, students not only gain recognition but also develop confidence, teamwork, and valuable real-world experience in media and communication.
